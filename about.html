I am a Chinese Researcher and Designer specializing in digital User Experience.

I follow Human-Centered Design approach for creating delightful experiences that balance people, business, and technology. In some cases, I am Self-Centered, and intuition is my best source for creativity.

Do
Good!

I wouldn't describe myself as a so-called UX designer, or whatever limits me within a particular realm of Design. Instead, I consider myself a Human Designer for the sake of being a Homo Sapiens and my care about human society.

When I think of Design, I associate it with a social practice conducted by people in a specific society or community that holds unique values. As a human designer, I interact with people, learn from society, and embed values. Armed with the artistic sense and technical skills, I strive to Improve life quality, Enrich culture, and Empower individuals. Or in a nutshell, be ETHICAL and do GOOD!

Keep
Exploring!

I hold a bachelor's degree in Industrial Design and am now pursuing an M.sc in Design for Interaction at TU Delft in the Netherlands.

At school, I am active in design seminars, cross-culture workshops, and student study trips. In July of 2019, I, together with other peer students, organized a trip (Flight Case) to Japan and collaborated with Ricoh, Panasonic, and DCM to inspire their employees and generate innovative solutions for their business.

Ever since I stepped into the world of Design, I was lucky to gain experiences at tech giants as UX Designer, Microsoft in 2018, and Alibaba in 2019. Also, in agile enviornments such as agency Wild Design (2019) and tech start-up myTomorrows (2020).

These experiences expanded my scope of knowledge and, more importantly, motivated me to keep exploring in the design field.
